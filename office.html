Our Practice

Thoughtful design at every scale

Studio Forma was founded in 2012 by Elena Marsh and David Kim with the conviction that good design is not a luxury — it is a fundamental quality of a well-lived environment. The practice operates across residential, cultural, and institutional commissions, drawing on a shared sensibility rooted in material honesty, spatial clarity, and contextual responsiveness.

Our process begins with listening. We work closely with clients to understand not just the functional requirements of a project, but the deeper aspirations — the quality of light desired, the relationship to the landscape, the way the space should feel at six in the morning when the house is quiet.

From these conversations, we build an architecture of specificity: buildings and interiors that are unmistakably suited to their particular place, program, and inhabitants. We do not have a house style. Each project earns its own identity through rigorous engagement with its conditions.
