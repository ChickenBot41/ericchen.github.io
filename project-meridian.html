Overview

Meridian Loft is a full gut renovation of a 2,800-square-foot apartment in a cast iron building in Manhattan's SoHo neighborhood. The clients — a couple with a young family — sought a home that honored the building's historic character while making room for a thoroughly contemporary way of living.


We stripped the apartment back to its bones, revealing the original pine floors, exposed steel columns, and vaulted brick ceilings that give the space its character. Against this raw, layered backdrop, we introduced a material palette of white plaster, oiled oak, and patinated bronze — surfaces that age gracefully alongside the building.


The kitchen anchors the open plan with a single marble-clad island that doubles as dining table and workspace, drawing the family together at the heart of the apartment.
